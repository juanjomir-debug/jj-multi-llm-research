🤖 How AI providers use your data

When you submit a query, the text is forwarded server-side to the AI model providers you select (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, xAI, Alibaba). Each provider processes your query text under their own API terms. All providers prohibit using API inputs to train their models.

  • Your email, username, and account data are never sent to AI providers
  • All connections use HTTPS/TLS 1.2+
  • Requests are made from our server — your IP is not exposed to providers

🍪 Cookies

We use a single session cookie to keep you authenticated. This cookie is:

  • HttpOnly — not accessible by JavaScript
  • Secure — only transmitted over HTTPS
  • SameSite: Lax — protects against cross-site request forgery

We do not use analytics cookies, advertising cookies, or any third-party tracking scripts.

🇪🇺 Your rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Erasure — request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Portability — export your query history from the app
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate personal information
  • Restriction — request we limit processing of your data
  • Objection — opt out of any processing based on legitimate interest
To exercise any of these rights, email info@reliableai.net with subject "GDPR request". We respond within 72 hours and complete data deletion within 30 days.

📱 iOS app

The ReliableAI iOS app is a native wrapper (built with Capacitor) that loads reliableai.net in a secure WebView. All data handling is governed by this same Privacy Policy. The app does not access your device's contacts, photos, location, microphone, camera, or health data. No data is stored locally on your device beyond the session cookie managed by the WebView.

👶 Children's privacy

ReliableAI is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us at info@reliableai.net and we will delete it promptly.
